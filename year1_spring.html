Year 1 - Quarter 3

Spring 2022

ATMS111: Global Warming

An exploration of causes and effects of climate change through atmospheric sciences. Analyzing current and possible solutions of mitigating harmful effects of global warming.

CHSTU200: Latinos in the United States

Studies broad patterns of inequality formed by historical forces, race, ethnicity, nationality, gender, immigration, and social capital amongst the Chicano community.

HONORS394: Community Inclusion and Equity in the Changing Public Realm

Examines the underpinnings of urban place making/place-keeping in local communities utilizing a framework of social justice, inclusion and equity.

Music116: Elementary Music Theory

Learning of basic skills in reading and notating music. As well as major topics like: pitch, rhythm, scales, intervals, counterpoint, chords, and harmony.
