SOC 110: Introductory course to Sociology

Purpose and Destiny

Reflection

One of the most impactful lectures from this course was the concept of socialization. Socialization is the process in which people internalize the shared values, norms, and behaviors of our society that are mutually agreed upon. This allowed me to see education through a sociological lens because schools enforce American values and norms to children. I thought this was a really interesting concept and allowed me to make deeper connections in my other courses about what values and norms were being pushed through our institutions. Overall, this class shifted my perspective of my studies and has allowed me to view education from a sociological lens.
