Educ251: Seeking Educational Equity and Diversity

legacy and memory

Reflection

EDUC251 is an infamous ECO course known for the incredible professor, Jondou Chen, and the impactful course content. Throughout each week, we explore a different aspect of intersectional identities and give space to share those stories. However, I am someone who had to reflect on my identities earlier in my life simply because it was necessary to make sense of my place within a white-centered education system and culture. I did want to share one of the most memorable moments I have from this course which was when I first discovered Carmen Lomas Garza’s artwork. It was a familiar feeling and it reminded me of the art pieces I would see in Mexico or El Salvador. I was able to connect with the art through my own experiences despite this being a collective memory of the Latine community. I adore Lomas Garza’s work and it’s always worthwhile to reshare our connection to art.
