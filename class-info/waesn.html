UCBI: Undergraduate Community-Based Internships

intentional

Reflection

UCBI was the first activity I participated in during my undergraduate experience. This two-quarter long internship paired students with a partner organization, provided mentorship, and professional development. As one of two freshmen in the cohort, I felt that I had a lot to learn and prove to myself that I belonged to take on this internship. My mentor, Susan, and my group mates were so supportive every step of the way. They offered advice on boundary setting, work-life balances, and just introduced me to new spaces in Seattle! And of course, this experience allowed me to take on the role of Elementary Intern at Canopy Scholars and stay involved for years to come. As an Elementary Intern, I supported the management of the elementary room, assisting tutors and students with homework help, and organizing daily materials. There are a handful of memorable moments during my first year at Canopy.
