PoliSci202: Introduction to American Politics

Love for Learning

Reflection

PolSci202 was the first ever Political Science course I took at the UW and it fueled my interest in studying political institutions. I’ve always enjoyed history as a subject which aligned with the course’s objective of providing a timeline of American political institutions. I was very dedicated to this course which allowed me to create healthy study habits like: annotating class readings, frequently reviewing notes, and actively participating in the quiz section. This dedication had positive consequences on my performance on exams which left me feeling like my hard work was paying off.
